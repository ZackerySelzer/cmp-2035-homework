Roast Beef Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds of roast beef
  • 1 sliced carrot
  • 1 sliced onion
  • 1 sliced garlic
  • 2 tables spoons soy sauce
  • 1 cup lager beer

Steps

  1. Mix marinade and pour over roast beef
  2. Marinade 3-4 hours
  3. Brown on hot frying pan on all side
  4. Pour marindae over roast placed in the roasting pan
  5. Roast in oven for 35-40 minutes
  6. Wrap in aluminum foil and let it rest for 10 minutes
